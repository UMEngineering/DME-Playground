Your Admissions Checklist includes the list of task needed to enroll at Michigan Engineering. Click each box to check when complete.

STEP ONE
Receive your acceptance letter and view admitted student website.

STEP TWO
Register for Campus Day. This program is held January through April, and registration starts in January.

STEP THREE
Complete your CSS Profile and FAFSA: The early deadline is February 15. April 30 is the final deadline.

STEP FOUR
Pay your $200 enrollment deposit. Deposit is due by May 1. Payment can be made via Wolverine Access, or post marked by May 1, 2013. Please login to Wolverine Access for more information about making your deposit.

STEP FIVE
Check scholarship status. Admitted first-year students are automatically considered for College of Engineering merit-based scholarships, and notification of awards are made before mid-April. U-M Scholarships are administered by the Office of Financial Aid and most recipients are notified by April 15.

STEP SIX
Submit your Housing/Michigan Learning Community Application. Due by May 7. If applying for a Michigan Learning Community, additional essays are required. Please verify the requirements for your selection. (Note: Enrollment Deposit must be paid)

STEP SEVEN
Register for Orientation. Registration slots made available in early April (Note: Enrollment Deposit must be paid)
