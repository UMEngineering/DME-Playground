beyond the classroom

To fine-tune the video games and move them closer to the hands of the populations they could help, a group of students from computer science and engineering instructor David Chesney's freshman and senior classes worked together through the summer. In addition to collaborating with the U-M Health Systems Mott Family Network to make games from both classes available to hospital patients, the young engineers put their products to the test with several groups of potential users.
