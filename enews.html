Cheers for Mars Science Lab landing

At the U-M landing party hosted by the Atmospheric, Oceanic and Space Sciences department and the Space Physics Research Lab, attendees erupted in applause and laughter when they got word that the Curiosity rover arrived safely.



 

Solar Car wins 4th straight National Championship

The University of Michigan Solar Car Team has won the 2012 American Solar Challenge for a 4th consecutive American title – and broken the national record.

 



 

Rankings: U-M is a global powerhouse

The University of Michigan is one of the top-ranked "Engineering/Technology and Computer Sciences" universities in the world, according to Shanghai Jiao Tong University in its annual global university ranking.



 

Student autonomous vehicle team wins gold

In a field of more than twenty competitors, the U-M student team won first place at the 2012 International Robotics Competition, beating schools such as Georgia Tech and Embry-Riddle with its custom-built quadrotor.



 

Joint biomedical engineering department holds promise for better health care technologies

In an effort to develop more technologies that improve health care, the University of Michigan will establish a joint Department of Biomedical Engineering with footholds in its College of Engineering and Medical School.
